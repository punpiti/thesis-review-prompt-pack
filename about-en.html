What This Site Reviews

Research Logic

Checks whether problem, objectives, method, and results actually connect.

References

Checks reference quality, missing citations, uncited entries, metadata, and citations that do not support claims.

Evidence

Checks dataset, baseline, metric, raw result, script, error analysis, and measurement support behind claims.

Ethics / IRB / Legal

Flags human subjects, personal data, consent, scraping, dataset license, copyright, Terms of Service, and NDA issues.

AI Use

Checks whether AI use may have introduced unsupported claims, suspicious citations, or unverified results.

Writing Hygiene

Checks typos, acronyms, Thai/English mixing, forward references, and generic AI writing.

Privacy and Limits

This static site does not collect research files.

These HTML pages are a public prompt list. They have no upload form, no database, and no server-side storage for user research content.

AI is a review assistant, not the thesis author, advisor, real reviewer, IRB office, or legal/compliance authority. Authors must verify every claim from real sources.

Do not let AI invent citations, DOI, venue rankings, metrics, results, IRB/legal requirements, or legal statements.
